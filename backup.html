Wait For Backup

You wait for backup outside the house, as the sounds of siren draws close you hear another shot.

As you and the others clear the building what you find just keeps getting worse and worse.

A woman in the living room with a bullet in her abdomen led in shattered glass that used to be a coffee table, dead from her wounds.

A man in the basement dead on the floor with a gun by his side

and lastly a young boy in his room, hiding under his bed, confused and crying.
